About ISM T!mes

Welcome to ISM T!mes, your premier source for t!mely news and updates from the Indian Institute of Technology (Indian School of Mines), Dhanbad. As the official campus newspaper, we bring you comprehensive coverage of academic achievements, research breakthroughs, cultural events, sports triumphs, and everything that shapes the vibrant life at IIT ISM Dhanbad.

Founded with the vision of keeping the campus community informed and connected, ISM T!mes serves as a bridge between students, faculty, and administration, ensuring that no significant development goes unnoticed in our prestigious institution.
